Amazon Product Sales Analysis

1.	Objective
Conduct annual sales analysis to provide insights to stakeholders and management to take strategic decisions to increase sales volume next year.


2.	Goal
-	Measure annual revenue, product reviews and sales
-	Measure last quarter revenue
-	Track monthly and weekly sales trend
-	Annual and last quarter sales as per product category to see category performance
-	Highlight top 5 products by annual sales and product reviews


3.	Analysis done
- Pulled products data into Power BI.
- Used DAX and Time intelligence functions to measure KPIs.
- Created monthly and weekly sales chart to track sales trend.
- Created a table to see product category performance 
- Highlighted top 5 products using a chart to track revenue drivers


4.	Results 
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Successfully measured a strong year-to-date sales and customer reviews


5.	Insights
- Slow sales growth till mid-year then a steep increase in sales from August. Significant revenue spike in October and December peaking over $1M. Sharp rise in weekly sales from week 30 onwards.
- End-of-year period (Q4) drives the majority of annual sales – possibly due to holiday season shopping.
- Inventory levels of top products must be well maintained during the Q4 period to meet the strong customer demand.
