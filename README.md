Amazon Product Sales Analysis

1.	Objective
Conduct annual sales analysis to provide insights to stakeholders and management to take strategic decisions to increase sales volume next year.

2.	Goal
-	Measure annual revenue, product reviews and sales
-	Measure last quarter revenue
-	Track monthly and weekly sales trend
-	Annual and last quarter sales as per product category to see category performance
-	Highlight top 5 products by annual sales and product reviews

3.	Analysis done
1) Pulled products data into Power BI.
2) Used DAX and Time intelligence functions to measure KPIs.
3) Created monthly and weekly sales chart to track sales trend.
4) Created a table to see product category performance 
5) Highlighted top 5 products using a chart to track revenue drivers

4.	Results 
1) Built an interactive Power BI dashboard.
2) Successfully measured a strong year-to-date sales and customer reviews

5.	Insights
1) Slow sales growth till mid-year then a steep increase in sales from August. Significant revenue spike in October and December peaking over $1M. Sharp rise in weekly sales from week 30 onwards.
2) End-of-year period (Q4) drives the majority of annual sales – possibly due to holiday season shopping.
3) Inventory levels of top products must be well maintained during the Q4 period to meet the strong customer demand.
